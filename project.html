Deep Learning for Computer Vision, Speech, and Language

We will have a poster presentation on Dec 11, 7pm-10pm. This is to show case every team's project to the class, and the most popular project will be awarded to an Idol Award. See examples of project in the previous year.

Every team is required to submit a report:

  • 4 pages double column (in ICASSP format) or 8 pages single column (in NIPS format).
  • Reports will be reviewed as the same standard as top conferences (e.g., CVPR/ICCV/ECCV for vision, ICASSP/Interspeech for speech, ACL/NAACL/EMNLP for vision). We hope students can learn from the papers that they presented in the class .
  • If teachers agree that a report has the quality or potential of a top conference publication, all members of the team will receive a A+ as their final grade.
